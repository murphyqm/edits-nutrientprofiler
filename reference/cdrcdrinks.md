# Example dataset called CDRC Drinks

A handcrafted set of data that can be used as the input for
nutrientprofiler functions

## Usage

``` r
cdrcdrinks
```

## Format

`cdrcdrinks` \## A data frame of 10 rows and 21 columns:

- name:

  Name of product

- brand:

  Brand of product

- product_category:

  Category of product

- product_type:

  Type of product, i.e. Food or Drink

- food_type:

  Type of food, used for specific gravity conversion

- drink_format:

  Format of drink, used for specific gravity conversion

- drink_type:

  Type of drink, used for speciifc gravity conversion

- nutrition_info:

  How nutritional information is presented, for specific gravity
  conversion

- energy_measurement_kj:

  Energy measurement in KJ

- energy_measurement_kcal:

  Energy measurement in kcal

- sugar_measurement_g:

  Sugar content measured in in grams

- satfat_measurement_g:

  The saturated fat content measured in grams

- salt_measurement_g:

  The salt content measured in grams

- sodium_measurement_mg:

  The salt content measured in mg of sodium

- fibre_measurement_nsp:

  The fibtre measurement using NSP

- fibre_measurement_aoac:

  The fibre measurement using AOAC

- protein_measurement_g:

  The protein emasurement of the product

- fvn_measurement_percent:

  The percent of fruit/veg/nuts in the product

- weight_g:

  The mass of the product if solid

- volume_ml:

  The volume of the product if liquid

- volume_water_ml:

  The volume of water for diluted products
